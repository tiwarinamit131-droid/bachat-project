About BACHAT

BACHAT is a web-based group expense management tool designed for students, friends, roommates, and trip groups. It helps you split expenses, track balances, and manage group finances easily and transparently.

  • Purpose: Make shared expense management simple and stress-free.
  • Target Users: Students, friends, roommates, travel groups.
  • Features: Group creation, expense split, analytics, Bachat Tips, Bachat Score, reminders, and more.

BACHAT encourages mindful spending and financial discipline with a clean, modern, and responsive interface.
